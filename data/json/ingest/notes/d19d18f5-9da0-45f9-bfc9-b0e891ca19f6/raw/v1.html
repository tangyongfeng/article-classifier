Freund vs. Freunde vs. Freundin vs. Freundinnen


Male friend vs. friends vs. female friend vs. female friends

That is accurate. Here's how it works:
SingularPlural
Nominativder Freunddie Freunde
Genitivdes Freund(e)sder Freunde
Dativdem Freundden Freunden
Akkusativden Freunddie Freunde
SingularPlural
Nominativdie Freundindie Freundinnen
Genitivder Freundinder Freundinnen
Dativder Freundinden Freundinnen
Akkusativdie Freundindie Freundinnen
  • Additional notes: for mixed-gender groups, use the male plural variants (that's called "generic masculine") or say something along the lines Freunde und Freundinnen to include female friends explicitly. The female plural variants refer to all-female groups only.
  • Add the appropriate possessive determiner (mein, dein, ihr, sein) in order to indicate that this a "friend-friend" since Germans don't have separate word for girlfriend or boyfriend. This is not unambiguous though. Sometimes mein Freund is just a regular friend.
