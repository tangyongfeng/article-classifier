Title
Transforming from Traditional Architecture to Mobile Internet oriented Architecture based on WebSphere Transaction Cluster Facility

Speaker 
Takao Inouye (IBM), Lan Shao (CLO), Cheng-Hui Luo (CLO)

Abstract
China's lottery market has expanded quickly. As lottery solution Operators provider, China Lottery Online company (CLO) is planning to transform their traditional architecture t IMPACT.docxo mobile internet oriented architecture, since many lottery ticket buyers have the willingness to make the transaction via the mobile internet for convenience, which has huge business potential. As mobile continues to keep us more connected, CLO has seized the opportunity to take industry trends and turn this into innovation.
In this joint session, CLO will describe how extending the enterprise to Mobile is imperative for their business.  To support mobile internet ticket sales, CLO is facing technical challenges based on their transitional architecture, e.g. extreme high volume players (400 million plays in total), extreme high performance and high availability requirements. Meanwhile, system scalability is becoming a very important factor since player volume will be extreme high at peak time via internet and social media. To respond to these challenges, CLO is building a new generation high volume transaction platform to solve these problems and support mobile internet lottery sales.
As a new technology, IBM WebSphere Transaction Cluster Facility (WTCF) is built on DB2 shared data technology – DB2 pureScale, and optimized for mission critical application that requires a centralized and consistent database, providing object-oriented interface, easy to develop new applications and re-engineer existing application in C++ and WAS using Java, and effortlessly scaling from hundreds to thousands to millions of transaction per second. The session will introduce how CLO successfully optimized their applications for the mobile experience and ingeniously designed a high volume transaction architecture to reduce costs and increase revenue through mobile using WTCF while demonstrating high performance and scalability. For CLO, WTCF has  proved to be a key driver for greater business value and agility.
