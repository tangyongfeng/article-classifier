Hast du deine Hausübungen gemacht? - Schon längst.
Did you do your homework? - For a long time. (already finished before 2 hours)
表示很久了，但是还是做了。
Wie lang wartest du schon? - Schon lange.
How long are you already waiting? - Already for a long time. (no information about how long)
表示很久。
