Freund vs. Freunde vs. Freundin vs. Freundinnen
Male friend vs. friends vs. female friend vs. female friends
That is accurate. Here's how it works:
Singular
Plural
Nominativ
der Freund
die Freunde
Genitiv
des Freund(e)s
der Freunde
Dativ
dem Freund
den Freunden
Akkusativ
den Freund
die Freunde
Singular
Plural
Nominativ
die Freundin
die Freundinnen
Genitiv
der Freundin
der Freundinnen
Dativ
der Freundin
den Freundinnen
Akkusativ
die Freundin
die Freundinnen

Additional notes: for mixed-gender groups, use the male plural variants (that's called "generic masculine") or say something along the lines
Freunde und Freundinnen
 to include female friends explicitly. The female plural variants refer to all-female groups only.
Add the appropriate possessive determiner (
mein, dein, ihr, sein
) in order to indicate that this a "friend-friend" since Germans don't have separate word for
girlfriend
 or
boyfriend
. This is not unambiguous though. Sometimes
mein Freund
 is just a regular friend.
